Who Am I?

Kevin! I'm a bit of a tech enthusiast engrossed and captivated by the field of I.T.

While there's a lot encompassed under that subject, I find myself particularly interested in programming - A passion that started in high school, all in front of a very old Dell monitor displaying some version of Eclipse, allowing me to program a video game in Java.

My creations

While I've worked on several personal projects, I began producing more presentable projects over the past few years. Here are a few highlights of those projects:

Malevolence
https://github.com/KevinAlexV/theMalevolence A turn-based card game built with a team of 4 in Unity. I designed the UI, wrote the story and acted as a playtester and bug-fixer for our core systems, without a specific understanding of each component.
Ghost Walker
https://github.com/KevinAlexV/GhostWalker A narrative-driven game built in Unity with a team of 5. I wrote the story, designed most of the levels and acted as project lead to ensure components connected and flowed together.
Child's Continuance
A short story following a child, Zach, and his obsession with space. It follows the theme "Dangers of naivety". I wrote this story to demonstrate a splitting narrative in a quick prototype before implementing it in a fully-fledged game.
MonoChrome/BlueDemise
https://github.com/BTechTeamRed/GameEngine
The Physics of a Web-swing
https://github.com/KevinAlexV/Web-Swing-Physics This is a simulation of the physics behind Spider-man's webswing, built in a team of 2 in the Unity game engine. The intent was to showcase how quick Spider-man can feasibly move by swinging across a city versus running, in which we use Usain Bolt as a comparison.
Geographic Info System
https://github.com/KevinAlexV/Geographic-Information-System A system that allows for the indexing and searching of several world coordinates from several databases. The program is interacted with via a script file, and is optimized for searches in large datasets. Using quick file parsing and efficient memory management.
Key data structures involved include Quad trees, Hash Tables, regex, and buffer pools.
VPN Email Scripts
Musical AI
This is a project revolving around the concept of AI changing their behaviour based on the tempo and rythym of the music playing in the background. The hope is to explore the many applications of an AI designed in this way, whether it be for a game mechanic or to help developers adjust the tone of their game by simply changing the music. This is being built in Unreal 5 with Blueprints and C++, and will be completed in April 2023.
